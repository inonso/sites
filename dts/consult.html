Consulting

CONSULTING SERVICES

Let our experience work for you!

With their extensive experience in the telecommunications industry, the founders of Diversified Telecom recognized the need for more than just affordable technical expertise on specific systems. The dynamic changes currently taking place in the telecommunications industry demand an ever-expanding knowledge of a wide variety of data and telecommunications products. These products have become vital to every company functioning in today’s modern business world. For a company to interact with any entity outside itself (including customers), it must have a means of communication. To this end Diversified Telecom Services works with its clients to provide DRPs (Disaster Recovery Plans) and BCPs (Business Continuity Plans) for network and voice infrastructure. Without efficient means of communications, no company can reach its full productive potential. Diversified Telecom can provide knowledge of the equipment and service necessary for any company to fully realize this potential.

Diversified Telecom’s technicians have numerous certifications in structured cabling and the telecommunications industries. These certifications include: State Electrical Specialty Certification, Net+, and various VoIP certifications (with over 20 years of experience). Diversified Telecom’s first priority in business is ensuring your network stays up so your business never goes down.

Diversified Telecom has been in business for over 30 years. Our Consulting Services include design, implementation, and support for your complete network infrastructure. Together we will setup the perfect system optimized for your unique needs.

About

Diversified Telephone Services, Inc. was established in Sarasota, Florida, in 1984. Its primary goal was to provide the highest quality telecommunications products and service available, to meet all of the customer’s communications needs. In 1998 the company’s name was changed to Diversified Telecom Inc., to reflect an expanded range of capabilities.
