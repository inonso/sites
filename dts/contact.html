About

Diversified Telephone Services, Inc. was established in Sarasota, Florida, in 1984. Its primary goal was to provide the highest quality telecommunications products and service available, to meet all of the customer’s communications needs. In 1998 the company’s name was changed to Diversified Telecom Inc., to reflect an expanded range of capabilities.
